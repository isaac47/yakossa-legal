# Data Deletion Request

**Yakossa**
*Last updated: February 25, 2026*

## Delete Your Account and Data

You have the right to request the deletion of your Yakossa account and all associated personal data at any time.

## What Gets Deleted

When you request account deletion, we permanently remove the following data:

- **Profile information**: Name, email, phone number, date of birth, profile photo
- **User content**: Reviews, ratings, photos, and comments
- **Location history**: All stored location data
- **Preferences**: Saved venues, favorites, and notification settings
- **Usage data**: App activity and interaction history

## What May Be Retained

In accordance with legal obligations, we may retain limited data for up to 90 days after your request:

- **Transaction records**: If applicable, for tax and accounting compliance
- **Fraud prevention data**: Anonymized identifiers to prevent abuse
- **Legal holds**: Data subject to ongoing legal proceedings or regulatory requirements

All retained data is either anonymized or deleted at the end of the retention period.

## How to Request Deletion

### Option 1 — In the App

1. Open Yakossa
2. Go to **Profile** → **Settings** → **Account**
3. Tap **Delete my account**
4. Confirm your identity
5. Your account and data will be permanently deleted within 30 days

### Option 2 — By Email

Send an email to **privacy@yakossa.com** with:

- **Subject**: Data Deletion Request
- **Body**: Include the email address or phone number associated with your Yakossa account

We will verify your identity and process your request within 30 days. You will receive a confirmation email once deletion is complete.

## Important Notes

- Account deletion is **permanent and irreversible**. You will not be able to recover your account, content, or data.
- If you are a **venue owner**, deleting your account will also remove your venue listing(s) from the platform.
- Active subscriptions should be cancelled through the App Store or Google Play before requesting deletion. Deleting your Yakossa account does not automatically cancel external subscriptions.
